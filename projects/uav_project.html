UAV Project

Project Overview

The UAV project is a concept whereas the endurance of the UAV is extended by docking with a quadcopter in mid-air and swapping out its depleted batteries for fresh ones. The project covers a range of disciplines including aircraft design, mechanical design, electrical design, PCB design, electronics integration, autonomous control, stereo camera vision, and machine learning.
